  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

How to Make Lasagna Step-by-Step

Here's a very concise explanation on your journey into achieving the perfect lasagna

  1. Cook the meat with seasonings and sauce.
  2. Cook them noodles.
  3. Make your ricotta mixture or if you're fancy, make your Bechamel sauce.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to your instinct
  5. Bake covered with foil
  6. Let the lasagna rest before devouring this masterpiece
